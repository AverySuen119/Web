With Compassion

We Create Change

"When we save stray animals, we are actually saving our own humanity. Every act of kindness is a redemption of civilization itself."

—— A volunteer’s reflections

A society's attitude towards animals reflects the society's civilization and moral standards. The stray animal problem is not an animal problem, but a microcosm of human society.

Every life saved tells us: Love has no boundaries, compassion has no conditions. Only when we learn to treat the weakest lives with gentleness can we truly understand the meaning of life.

Our characteristics and mission

Animal Rescue

We organize volunteers to carry out rescue operations to provide assistance to stray and injured animals.

Adoption Activities

Find loving homes for pets by holding regular adoption days.

Education and publicity

We promote animal protection awareness and call on the public to care for and respect life.
